How long will it take to deliver my order?

all orders by default qualify for 24-hours delivery from the time of placing your order online and 1-hour delivery if you have placed an urgent order via our free number (8005925634). while we try to ensure that we deliver within the promised time, all orders are subject to slight delays during peak/ sale periods etc. in such as case, you will receive a call from our agent to inform you about the same.

What payment methods can I use?

We accept all major bank cards online and you can even choose to pay ‘Cash on delivery’ or ‘Card on delivery’. Our driver will carry a card machine (or change in cash) if you have requested for the same at the time of placing your order.

How can I reorder?

you can reorder any previously ordered product(s) by accessing your purchase history in your account section after signing in.

What to do if I received a damaged/ defective/ incorrect product?

Our brands & partners take tested control measures to ensure that our products are of the highest quality and reach you in the right condition. However, in case the product has arrived damaged, defective or incorrect, please go to the nearest BinSina Pharmacy to report this or contact us immediately through the options on the contact us page. The item must be returned in the same condition you received it in within 3 days of receipt. Replacement may be available. Defective items may also benefit from a manufacturer’s defects warranty.

How can I get more information?

In case you need more information on anything, please reach out to us using any of the channels mentioned on the contact us page as per your convenience.
